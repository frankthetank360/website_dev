About Me

Graduated with my Masters in electrical engineering in 2013 from UNH, after I successfully defended my Master's Thesis: "Intelligent Aerial Store and Forward Packet Repeater". I have five years of experience in the defense industry, where I worked with a variety of technologies, including electronic warefare, thermal imaging and laser countermeasures. I have extensive experience designing printed circuit boards, coding embedded C/C++, working with VHDL, testing electronics in the lab and integrating commercial hardware with custom designs. I enjoy activities such as surfing, rock climbing, mountain biking, hiking and photography; so much so that I took a gap year in 2017 to pursue these hobbies full time and give myself space to plan where I want to go next with my life. At the conclusion of my trip I had a very different perspective on life and my priorities had shuffled drastically. The resultant of this fundamental shift in my personal beliefs galvanized me to return to academics, reinvent myself and change my current life trajectory. More than anything I want to return to school to learn as much as possible, work on exciting research and use this new knowledge to teach and excite others.
